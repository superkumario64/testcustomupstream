Welcome!

The Psychology department is home to some of the most creative faculty and unique research programs in the country. Also, most of our faculty are award-winning instructors! For more information, please go to our faculty web page.

Our department administers the undergraduate Psychology major, the undergraduate Cognitive Science major, and three graduate programs in psychology.
